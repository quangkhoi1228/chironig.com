About us

Chiron Investments Group (“CIG”) provides financial and investment advisory services to individual and institutional clients, focusing on the United State of America, Asia Pacific, and Vietnam markets.

CIG’s management and professional team have intensive experiences with over 20 years in Asia Pacific and nearly 30 years in America. Our profound track records inclusive of investment banking, commercial banking, corporate finance, asset management, real estate, auditing, merger and acquisition (M&A), insurance, and garment and textile with transactions accross the covered markets.

Our headquarter is in California, USA, and representative office is in Saigon, Vietnam.

Vision and mission

At Chiron Investments Group, our vision is being the strategic and distinguished financial advisor to our individual and institutional clients, assisting our clients in realizing their target with the highest dedication and integrity standard.

Our mission is being the premier advisor for comprehensive financial solutions to our clients, accommodating diversified financial and investment strategies toward prudent and decent results. We always dedicate to our clients’ interests and strive to give back to our communities.

Services

Capital Markets

CIG provides financial and structuring advice in connection with capital raising alternatives, inclusive of debt, listed and private equity, and hybrid capital, and assists clients with identifying investors, developing transactions’ terms and structures adequately.

Buy side M&A

CIG dedicates time and resources to formalise a prudent structured process around the target, advising our clients step by step through every stage of the transaction.

Sell side M&A

CIG team will work hand in hand with clients through every stage of the transaction and navigate clients through the M&A process for maximizing the transaction’s value.

Restructuring and Liability Management

CIG provides comprehensive solutions for financial and capital restructuring in connection with stressed and distressed situations, inclusive of amendments, debt exchanges, financings, distressed M&A, and balance sheet recapitalizations.

Shareholder Engagement

CIG advises management team and Board of Directors on critical matters that require shareholders’ interaction and support, inclusive of shareholders’ votes on transactions and annual proxy matters, as well as shareholders’ activism preparation and managing a company campaign in response to an attempt by an activist to gain board control or influence.

Investor Relations

CIG advises clients with comprehensive investor relations strategy for managing company’s sustainable valuation.

Advisory Committee

CIG provides strategic advice to clients’s Board of Directors in connection with financial, structural, regulatory features of transactions where there is an actual, apparent or potential conflict.
